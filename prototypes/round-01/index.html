What We Do

Research Areas

PHI Lab bridges clinical informatics, AI, and epidemiology to generate evidence from real-world healthcare data.

Knowledge Representation

Clinical data modeling, ontology engineering, and end-to-end data pipeline construction for interoperable healthcare information.

Data Modeling Ontology Pipelines

Real-World Data (RWD)

Secondary analysis of electronic health records, clinical data warehouses, and FAERS for large-scale observational research.

EHR CDW FAERS

Real-World Evidence (RWE)

Rigorous scientific data processing and statistical frameworks to generate regulatory-grade clinical evidence from observational sources.

Causal Inference Evidence

Pharmacovigilance

Drug safety monitoring systems, adverse event signal detection, and post-market surveillance using spontaneous reporting databases.

Drug Safety Signal Detection

Healthcare AI

Evaluation of large language models in clinical settings, named entity recognition, and deep learning for patient phenotyping.

LLM Clinical NER Deep Learning

Precision Medicine

Genomic data integration, multi-omics cancer trajectory analysis, and the MOMENTUM study for personalized breast cancer treatment.

Genomics Multi-Omics MOMENTUM
