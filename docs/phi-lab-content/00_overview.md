## 핵심 콘텐츠 요약

### 연구 3대 축 (Home)
1. **Knowledge Representation** — data modeling, engineering, pipeline construction, management & governance
2. **Real-World Data (RWD)** — secondary use of EHR/CDW, FAERS
3. **Real-World Evidence (RWE)** — scientific data processing for evidence generation

### 주요 연구 프로젝트 (Research)
- MOMENTUM Study (유방암 AI+RWD, 다기관, Kakao Healthcare·삼성서울병원)
- AI-Based Cancer Trajectory (NRF 지원, 대장암·유방암 표현형)
- LLM 성능 평가 + 임상 NER (Kakao Healthcare, 특허 출원)
- FAERS 중복제거 / 약물감시 프레임워크 (SJS-TEN, 과배란유도제 등)
- 소아 항생제 처방 → ADHD/ASD 위험 (가톨릭대 생의학)
- 사회결정요인 + 유방암 사망률 (Harvard, U. Cincinnati, Tufts, SKKU)

### 협력기관
Samsung Medical Center, Ewha Womans University, Jeonnam National University, Catholic University of Korea, Kakao Healthcare, U. Cincinnati, Harvard, Tufts, Sookmyung Women's University, Chonnam National University, Eunpyeong St. Mary's Hospital, Bucheon St. Mary's Hospital
