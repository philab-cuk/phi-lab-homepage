## Lab Description (English)

PHI (Precision & Provenance Health Informatics Lab) at the Catholic University of Korea is dedicated to advancing precision medicine and digital healthcare through data-driven interdisciplinary research. The lab's work spans from public data to EHR (Electronic Health Records) data, emphasizing the utilization of trustworthy data, knowledge generation via data science, and supporting decision-making through data-based digital healthcare systems. The lab collaborates with various medical institutions and schools both domestically and internationally to engage in vibrant research activities. These include engineering real-world data (RWD), constructing data pipelines, generating real-world evidence (RWE), and designing information structures to enhance healthcare delivery and outcomes. This comprehensive approach not only boosts the field of digital healthcare but also ensures that the solutions developed are grounded in reliable data and innovative research practices.
